Summary
Daniel
is overwhelmed by his problems and decides to run away. He returns home to
his mother
and discovers she is dating someone. Feeling like a burden he decides to live on the streets. Daniel realizes how difficult it is to be homeless.
While living on the streets, Daniel hears about a famous thief called 'the Mole' who steals people's things. In the subway he falls asleep and gets his money stolen. Daniel witnesses some homeless fighting over his wallet.

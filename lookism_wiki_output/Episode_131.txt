Summary
Logan rushes at Daniel and he dodges his attacks, finishing him with a sucker punch. Zoe comes in to see him on the ground and helps him up. Logan asks her to meet him in a store.
At the store, Logan asks Zoe to go out with him. She rejects him for his terrible personality and the way he treated everyone. Logan gets depressed and gains back all the weight he lost.

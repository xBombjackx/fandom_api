Summary
The trainee is
Joy Hong
, who he modeled with previously. Joy joined PTJ out of fun, and to see Daniel as well. The trainees go out to eat at a restaurant and Daniel finds out they are all on a budget. When the Class A kids leave their food, Daniel tries to take some and they make fun of him for it. Joy pays for everyone's food and tells them off. As he is heading home, Oliver spies a picture of the singer
Aru Lee
and sings to himself.
Daniel is heading to the agency when he meets the Class A boys and a new guy, who beckon him over.

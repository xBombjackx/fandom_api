Synopsis
Hostel (Arc)
Eli is introduced as an illegally sold child at a broker by Olly. He is later returned once the doctor diagnoses him as a psychopath. Later, he is adopted again by a family that wants to buy an apartment, then abandoned once the family gets their apartment. It is revealed that Eli has been adopted and returned 13 times in total. Eli thinks about how Olly is the only one that acts nice to him, except when someone named 'Eli Jang' is talked about. He goes to the convenience store to steal food, and is stopped by the real Eli, who he angrily confronts.

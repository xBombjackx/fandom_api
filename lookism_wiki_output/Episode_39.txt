Daniel
is studying for midterms when he realizes his second body isn't any smarter than his first. He decides to study in both bodies. First, he asks
Jay
for help since he is the smartest in the class. The class president (second ranked in the class) gets jealous of Daniel and tries to beat him in studying. Later he goes to the library to study with
Jiho
and
Duke
, who is also a smart student (the fifth ranked of his class). At the convenience store Daniel runs into
Zoe
, who comes over to flirt with him. She decides to help him study since she is the top student in math.

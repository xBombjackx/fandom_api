Jason Yoon (윤경헌 Yoon Kyung-heon)
is the no.3 of Big Deal.
Appearance
Jason is shown as a slim young man with hair parted on each side. He has a habit of lowering his face and staring at his opponent.
Jason also usually wears his Big Deal uniform, a black overcoat, a white dress shirt, black suit pants, and dress shoes, apparently without socks. He occasionally wears red high-top All-Star sneakers.

Synopsis
Background
Jason grew up on Jeju Island and trained in Taekwondo. He came to Seoul because he wanted to fight someone famous for his Taekwondo skills who is the same age as him. That person is implied to be
Seong Taehoon
, a character of Viral Hit, another manhwa in the same universe as Lookism.
[
10
]
He transferred to Gangseo Middle School because he heard it was the toughest and immediately started fighting the upperclassmen to decide the head of the school. During his fights he met
Brad Lee
who was doing the same thing and, after beating everyone else, they decided to fight each other for the position of head, but they were ambushed by the upperclassmen. They were both saved by
Jake Kim
and decided to follow him.
[
11
]
Juvenile Prison Jiho Park Arc
Jason Yoon served his prison sentence along with many other Big Deal members in the aftermath of the illegal gambling ring. During his time at juvenile prison he helped advice Jake Kim about choosing future members of Big Deal. He was disgusted by Jiho's tactics of winning against other cell leaders, and challenged him to win against him. Jiho confronted Jason at the woodshop, and convinced Wally to push Jason into a saw.
[
12
]
Jason missed the saw by inches. Jake later told him that Jason had lost against Jiho, and did so "fair and square."
[
13
]
Jake Kim Arc
Jason Yoon is introduced along with Brad Lee as bullies of C Middle School.
[
14
]
He became friends with Jake Kim after he helped them take down the middle school uprising. Jason Yoon swore to follow Jake Kim afterwards, and become his right-hand man eventually joining Big Deal.
Fighting Prowess
Jason is the best fighter from Jeju. His fighting style is Taekwondo. He relies on speed, and leg strength. He also knows Taekkyeon, according to him, his interest is in just kicking.
Quotes
"Sorry to disappoint, but i don't take inordinate pride in Taekowndo or anything like that... I just like kicking"
Trivia
He is known as "Jeju's Crimson Stallion" due to his powerful kicks, his red shoes and his hometown.

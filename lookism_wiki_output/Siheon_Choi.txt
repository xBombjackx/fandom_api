Appearance
Siheon is a man with black hair and eyes, a muscular body with tattoos of skulls on his arms of various animals, he wears a black shirt and a sleeveless black leather vest that exposes his arms, sunglasses and a chain necklace with a silver skull pendant.
Personality
Siheon, like the other Kings who gathered at Gitae Kim's request, is arrogant, considering himself above the other kings present and not bothering to openly insult them. Although he is confident in his strength, he recognizes that his men would not be a match for the Gentleman of Generation 0, as he knew of his strength in his time and warned them to avoid confrontation.

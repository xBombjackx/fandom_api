Summary
Logan wants to beat up Warren but after seeing his chivalry decides to let him go, saying "
ganbare
". Sally and Warren introduce themselves to the gang, and are surprised to find
Vasco
is a friend of theirs. They leave with those same words and meet Eli, who dyed his hair a mix of black and purple. They welcome him back into their family and catch up with each other.
Jerry Kwon's Performance Test
At Jerry Kwon's middle school his teacher is concerned about him. Jerry is an intimidating student, and she is afraid he will corrupt other students into gang activity. She already has to deal with the Monster crew, a band of delinquents at Jerry's middle school.
The Monster crew are led by Lineman, a senior student who is part of Big Deal. He lies about his status and claims to know Jerry Kwon, No. 2 of Big Deal. When he is collecting dues he comes across Jerry, who the students are scared to ask for money. To get his attention, Lineman breaks Jerry's flute and Jerry gets angry.

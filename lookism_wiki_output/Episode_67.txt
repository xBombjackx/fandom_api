Summary
Daehoon takes Doo and Taejoon to a construction site, and Vasco, Daniel and Zack decide to head there. They are shocked to find Daehoon is only acting, and has been using Doo and Taejoon to boost his followers. Vasco tells Taejoon to apologize and he will save him. Daehoon grabs Vasco by the collar and is shocked to see his tattoos.

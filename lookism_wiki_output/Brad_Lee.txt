Appearance
Brad Lee has a stong looking figure with a constant sharp posture. His head is completly shaven as his eyebrows are bushier and short. He has a tall height and is usualy seen wearing the casual
Big Deal
attire, White shirt, black open coat, black pants and black formal shoes.
Personality
Brad is a very dual person as he can completely change moods when necessary or when in shock, although most of the time he is seen cracking jokes. He takes things very serious when it comes to Jake. He holds Big Deal's ideology very close to him.

Summary
Gun
tells
Daniel
to stop investigating about James Lee, or he will make an enemy of the chairman. He agrees to tell Daniel about James Lee if he is willing to start a crew, even going so far as to threaten his friends to work under him. He offers him money and power, but Daniel refuses and Gun decides to kill him. As Gun is choking Daniel, he becomes unconscious, allowing Daniel to tap into UI for the 2nd time and begins fighting Gun. He overwhelms him until he wakes up suddenly, and was revealed to be winning against Gun.

Summary
Daniel begins copying Zack and
Vasco's
moves but he is overwhelmed by Logan. Despite his weaker physique he continues to fight until Logan hits him with his right hand. He passes out.
Daniel is in the convenience store when he sees all his old friends. His second body comes in and walks away with them, and he is sad to be left behind. Then he sees his old body come to him and ask why he can't approach his friends first. He races to catch them and thanks his old self, but it has disappeared. He smiles and realizes he had to leave his old self behind before he could change into his better self. With this, he wakes up in his second body.

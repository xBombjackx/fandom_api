Seonong Ham
is a minor character who attends Potato High School. He is a large, scary looking teen who is in love with
Soomi
. He first appears in the Picnic arc, where he tries to impress Soomi by being rude to the
J High
boys. The joke is on him when he gets beaten by every one of them except
Daniel.
During his stay, Seonong managed to trick
Vasco
into thinking he owes him some money. He returns to Seoul to give him back his money, and meets
Brekdak
. The two of them open shop in Seoul to earn enough money to pay Vasco back.

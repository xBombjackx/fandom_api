Mujin Jin
is a minor character in Lookism as the father of Vin Jin and master of 1st Generation King, Seongji Yuk.
Appearance
Mujin Jin is an incredibly muscular man, with messy black hair typically styled into a mullet shaved on the sides. He has been shown to only wear a pair of blue shorts with a red strip of fabric, presumably the one his wife would tie her hair with, around his waist. His face is covered in a black shadow, with his left eye emitting a white glow, similar to when
Vin Jin
hid his polycoria.
It's possible that his son's polycoria was inherited from Mujin.
Personality
He is said to have lived his entire life in the wild.

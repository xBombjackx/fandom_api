Inu (Enu)
is a supporting character of
Lookism
. Inu is a female crossbred of a Labrador retriever and an unknown breed.
Appearance
Inu resembles her mother, a yellow Labrador retriever. She has thick eyebrows, black eyes, and usually wears a red scarf her original owner gave to her.
Personality
Inu is very loyal to loved ones, even going to the point of attacking others. Before being fond of Daniel, she would often retain an aggressive manner, even biting Crystal's leg and Daniel.

Background
She was abandoned by her original owner, "Byeol," because she was not a purebred, therefore others were shaming Inu. Before she became fond of
Daniel
, she was very aggressive, biting
Crystal
. After she had her 4 puppies, she then became fond of Daniel and Jay.
Plot
First introduced in chapter 58, where her past was shown as being a crossbred, she was viewed as disgraceful to her family, even being called a "mongrel". She was optimistic, convinced her master would someday return. The original Daniel made a temporary cardboard box house to shelter her from the rain, he even washed her scarf and gave her seaweed soup. At this point, Inu became fond of Daniel.
Jae Yeol
and Inu's Puppies Spin-Off
Jae was shown walking Inu's puppies after his butler forced him to, while walking them in the park, Jae encounters Daniel running with Inu. Later, Jae Yeol ties his puppies to a pole to saftely pick up droppings one of the puppies left. When he turns around, he realizes that the puppies have chewed of their leashes. He runs into Daniel again. both in panic after they both lost their dogs. The puppies went off to an aggressive dog, when the aggressive dog attempts to bite one of the puppies, one of the siblings  jump in front, injuring himself. Inu later shows up trying to fend off the dog. After the problem finally resolves and they recover, Jae Yeol is later shown confidently walking the now-grown puppies. Jae even gives them luxury clothing, and he ties their leashes to his belt.

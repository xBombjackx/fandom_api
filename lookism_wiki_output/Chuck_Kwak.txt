Chuck Kwak is a minor character in Lookism.
Appearance
Justin and Chuck both have black hair and various scars on their faces. Chuck has long, messy black hair and a green striped shirt with a brown overcoat. He also has a tattoo of a diamond on his head, and a more angular face than Justin.
Personality
Justin and Chuck share similar personalities. They are both ruthless in their fighting and willing to employ any method to defeat their enemies, grabbing necklaces and double teaming. They don't believe in fair fighting, and claim that in a fight the only thing that matters is winning.

Kenta Magami (マガミ・ケンタ
Magami Kenta
)
also known as "
Daruma
" One of the executives of the 2nd affiliated company of the
Workers
, Oyabun of the Magami faction, a Japanese yakuza organization.
Appearance
Daruma is a young man who wears a black suit and a mask. He has a skeleton-shaped earring and a fade haircut, but there’s a scar on the top of his head and he has both of his pinky fingers missing.
Personality
Relationships
Gun Park
Kenta Magami saw Gun as his idol and sworn brother at first but now he sees him as his mortal enemy because Gun eradicated his clan and killed his father.
Synopsis
Initially, he was the only son of
Ryuujo Magami
, who led the Magami faction, but in the past, his father and members of the Magami faction were killed and brutally dismembered by
Gun Park
. Subsequently, after the restoration of the Magami faction destroyed by Gun Park,
Mitsuki Soma
, who came to Japan to recruit new fighters, told Kenta that Gun Park is in Korea, and went to Korea to get revenge on him.
Fighting Prowess
Matching the title of next-generation oni, superior combat power comparable to the
Four Major Crews
, great fighting scenes and stunts such as setting fire to his index finger right before fighting with
Zack Lee
, and when
Ryuhei Kuroda
flew away after being hit by
Xiaolong
, he calmly took off his mask. One scene appears in the work, for example, preparing for battle, holding on to the end without fainting even after losing a fight with
Jerry Kwon
, and getting out of the car in the episode with the
The Hunt for Big Deal
, warning his opponent,
Jason Yoon
, and then fighting with him on equal terms and subsequently defeating him.

The reason you can't beat me is because the man I'm trying to kill is stronger than you

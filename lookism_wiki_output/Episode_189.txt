Summary
Logan leads Daniel into an alley and begins harassing him. He asks for his number and Daniel lies about knowing, so he starts beating him. Zoe witnesses this and throws her shoe at Logan, yelling at him for being a bully.
Later, Logan goes back to the convenience store and beats Daniel again. He takes Daniel's phone and gets Zoe's number. He then tells her to meet him at night using Daniel's phone.

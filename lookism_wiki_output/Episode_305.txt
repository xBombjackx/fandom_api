Summary
When the Black Bear gang try to fight them, the kids decide to run, but they ran in the direction of the Black Bear gang's territory. They are caught and tied in the gang's pool as they interrogate them. Jason and Brad quickly confess to not being Big Deal, but the gang leader decides to kill them anyway to spare his reputation. Jake offers to go first, but before they load him into their van, Sinu Han appears to rescue them.

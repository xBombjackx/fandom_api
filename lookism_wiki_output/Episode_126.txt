Zack is depressed that is weaker than Logan and complains he was out of practice for too long. Mira defends Zack from Logan and he grabs her by the hair. Zack gets up fast and punches Logan.
Vasco interrupts the fight to tell them to get along. Logan flips Vasco and tries to twist his ankle but cannot because he is strong. Logan is reminded of
Goo Kim

Zack interrupts the fight to tell Vasco to back off. Vin appears to take revenge on Logan. Logan realizes he is cornered and challenges them all. They are stopped by the teacher, but rumors' spread about Logan's incredible strength.

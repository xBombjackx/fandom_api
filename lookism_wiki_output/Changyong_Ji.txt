Appearance
He has small eyes, long straight black hair that reaches his upper back and a knife scar running accross his left cheek. He wears a black, white striped suit, a black necktie and a white polo shirt underneath.
Personality
When not fighting, Changyong is polite and very articulate with his words. His politeness fades away however when he gets fired up in a fight, losing his politeness, but definitely not his articulation.

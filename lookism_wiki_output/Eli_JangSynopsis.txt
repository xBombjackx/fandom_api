Overview
As a child, Eli Jang lived in an abusive household. He ran away when he was 10 years old and met someone he referred to as
"Mister Carpenter"
who taught him how to survive alone on the streets and also some carpentry skills.

Some time afterwards he met Sally Park and her friends and decided to live in Gangdong with them. Together they formed Hostel, one of the
Big 4 Crew
, with Eli as their leader.
During his time in Hostel, Eli fell in love with a girl named
Heather
and conceived a child with her. When Heather died, Eli left Hostel to take care of his daughter, Yenna, and stopped fighting. It was shown that the reason he is interested in
Crystal Choi
(the original body) is because she reminded him of Heather.
Upon coming to J High, Eli became the crush of many beauty department girls, who complain that he disappears after school. The reason he avoids them is that he is very busy taking care of Yenna and working at his part-time job as a hairdresser.
Background
Eli Jang was subjected to a rough upbringing with his father abusing his mother. This led to his mother running away. Eli's father remarried, with the new wife beating him severely. Eli's father died from unknown circumstances with the new wife seemingly indifferent, implying she was involved with a life-insurance fraud involving Eli's father. In either case, the new wife remarried her old husband and brought in her biological children to Eli's household.
The mother continued her abuse and neglect of Eli, keeping him locked away whilst the rest of the family enjoyed their new dwelling. The only memorable interaction Eli had with his family was when he played a twisted version of hide-and-seek with his mother where once the hider was found, they would be severely beaten. This game warped Eli's conception of love with him coming to enjoy the game as this was the only time his new mother would come to seek him out. The culmination of Eli's childhood was his running away at the age of 10. On the streets, he was brought up by "Mr. Carpenter".

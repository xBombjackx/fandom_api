Yeonwoo Kim
(
김연우
) was a member of the
0th Generation
. She is the owner of Big Crabs Like Big Crabs store. She took Daniel Park when he was searching for the Red Paper in Cheonliang. She then revealed that she is the one who currently possesses the red paper and that Daniel was the nephew of Jinyeong Park.
She also has a group of men durable enough to survive the punches of Daniel Park and Vin Jin.
Appearance
Yeonwoo is a beautiful woman, she is very tall compared to Daniel's original body height. She has an hourglass figure, highlighted by a noticeably large bust and neck-length short curtain black hair, with noticeable abs
As the boss, she dresses suitably for her age, often opting for a cream corduroy bodycon dress paired with a coat with a snow leopard print. Completing her ensemble, she wears a pair of ruby earrings, a ruby necklace, a single bracelet on her left arm, and black high heels.
Personality
While specific details about Yeonwoo's personality are limited, her actions suggest she is resourceful, confident, and possesses a commanding presence. Her ability to lead and maintain a group of resilient individuals indicates strong leadership qualities.
Relationships
Jinyoung Park
Yeonwoo Kim seems to have a deep and complex relationship with Jinyoung Park, likely stemming from their time in the 0th Generation. When she saw him again, her expression was filled with concern and nostalgia, hinting at strong past feelings. Additionally, she wears a coat similar to his, possibly as a sign of admiration or a subconscious connection to him. While their exact relationship is not explicitly stated, there are hints that Yeonwoo may have loved Jinyoung at some point.

Summary
Oliver has a flashback to his relationship with Aru. Both were poor trainees struggling to make ends meet. Aru wanted to be his girlfriend but he refused because he had to focus in his career. When Aru opened up to him he started to care about her. He learned she was struggling in the agency and they started to rely on each other. Everything fell to pieces when Aru cheated on him.

Miro is
Johan Seong's
pet dog. She used to belong to a little girl and her mother when she was kidnapped by an animal abuser. Miro at the time was pregnant, and gave birth to puppies.
She was saved by
Daniel
and returned to her family, but the mother abandoned the dog when she saw her in a malnourished state. Johan Seong adopted her after finding her in the garbage. Since then she has followed him wherever he goes.

Summary
Sally tells Eli not to fight and Warren punches Eli. Sally then gets angry at Warren and tells him he is mean for hitting Eli. Warren walks away feeling hurt, but returns in the night claiming he will stay at Sally's so he can protect her. Sally protests but eventually allows him and his friends Max and Derrick to stay.
In a bathroom, Backdoor's leader
Darius Hong
is using the toilet when he hears that Warren Chae beat multiple members of Backdoor on his own. He decides to retaliate and asks if Warren is pretty.
In Sally's house, Max and Derrick explain their backstory to Sally. Max was abandoned in a clothing bin and Derrick was abandoned on a bus. Warren explains that he, Max and Derrick live in the same orphanage and that is how they met. Sally explains her backstory, that her parents died in an accident and left her the building. They ask Eli to explain his backstory, and he tells them that he killed his dad.

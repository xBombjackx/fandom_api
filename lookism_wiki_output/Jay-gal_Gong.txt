Jay-gal Gong
is a student in the Comics and Animation department.
Appearance
Jay-gal is a short kid who wears a bowl cut. He looks like an average high school student.
Personality
He is a troublemaker and a smoker who thinks he is above his classmates. Jay-gal despises the comics department because they are losers and get looked down upon.
Relationships
Logan Lee
When
Logan Lee
appears, Jay-Gal is amazed by his bad boy aesthetic. He becomes Logan's fanboy and instigates fights between him and students in the other departments. Logan does not seem to care for Jay-gal, and treats him like a nuisance.

Summary
Vasco is shocked to see Daniel can still fight in his drunk state. Cho Ma, a fashion sophomore, steps in to stop the fight.
Jace
tells Vasco to leave Daniel alone and he tells Daniel to meet him later.
Eli
remembers seeing Daniel at his salon late that night and wonders what kind of person Daniel really is.
Daniel spots Crystal and corners her in an alley. He asks why she is avoiding him and almost collapses on her. Eli restrains him and asks what he is doing to Crystal. Daniel passes out and wakes up in his original body.

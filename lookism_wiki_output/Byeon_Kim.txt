Byeon Kim is a minor character in Lookism. He is a member of the Ten Geniuses, though what he's the genius of is yet unknown.
Appearance
Byeon Kim is a well-built man likely in his 30s or 40s, with short, shaggy black hair and a thin short boxed beard. His face appears to be scarred, with one across his lips, one across his left eye, and one across his forehead and the bridge of his nose. He is shown wearing red sunglasses with silver frames and a suit.
Personality
Not much has been shown of his personality, but he seems to be well-versed in media and the law.
